About Sign-to-Text Recognition

The *Sign-to-Text Recognition System* is an AI-powered web application that translates sign language gestures into text and speech in real time. Using *MediaPipe Hands* and a trained machine learning model, this system detects hand gestures from a live video feed and converts them into corresponding letters (A-I).

Our Mission

Our goal is to bridge the communication gap between individuals using sign language and those who do not understand it. This system provides *real-time translation*, making interactions more accessible and inclusive.
